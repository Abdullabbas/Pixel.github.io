About Elden Ring

Set in the Lands Between, a mystical realm once ruled by Queen Marika. The Elden Ring, source of the Erdtree’s power, has been shattered, sparking a war known as The Shattering. Players take the role of a Tarnished, exiled warriors guided back by grace to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord. Narrative combines mythic lore by Martin with Miyazaki’s signature cryptic storytelling. Gameplay Features Open World Exploration: Vast landscapes including grassy plains, swamps, mountains, castles, and dungeons. Traversal: On foot or horseback using Torrent, the spectral steed. Combat: Wide variety of weapons, spells, and skills. Options for direct combat, stealth, or summoning allies. Progression: Players collect Runes (currency) to level up attributes. Multiplayer: Cooperative boss fights and PvP duels. Reception & Legacy Awards: Winner of multiple Game of the Year honors, praised for its open world, freedom, and depth. Sales: Over 30 million copies sold, making it one of the best-selling games ever. DLC: Shadow of the Erdtree (June 2024) — expanded lore, new bosses, and regions. Sold over 10 million copies, even nominated for Game of the Year. Spinoff: Elden Ring: Nightreign (2025), focused on multiplayer. Future: A film adaptation is in production, slated for release in 2028. Risks & Considerations Difficulty: Known for punishing combat; not beginner-friendly. Online Play: PvP can be unbalanced, and cooperative play requires coordination. Time Investment: Exploration and progression demand patience and persistence.
