About Horizon Zero Dawn

In an era where colossal, animal-like machines roam a vibrant, post-apocalyptic wilderness, humanity has regressed into fractured, primitive tribes. You step into the role of Aloy, a skilled young hunter and outcast, as she embarks on a deeply personal quest to uncover her origins and the secrets of a vanished civilization.
